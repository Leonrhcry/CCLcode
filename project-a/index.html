[ Virbra]

Scientific Name:

SCP-XXXX

Discovered by / at:

A Meteorite fell in the Sahara

Family:

A once prosperous species lived in a planet with ample light. But the overabsorption of light destroyed their galaxy only few of them survived. The remainders now wonder through space.

Habitat:

Absorbing surrounding light sources and creating an expanding zone of darkness, which is the hunting area.

Appearance:

Black; star-shaped; having distinguishable facial features, including rudimentary eyes and a mouth

Key Characteristics:

  • Photophilic behavior
  • Ability of absorbing light
  • Evolve as the intensity of light increases
  • Aggressive and intelligent

What we know so far about SCP-XXXX? Through previous research, we have discovered that the dark zone expanded by SCP-XXXX will disappear after some time. Light source not only provide it with energy, but also makes it evolve. SCP-XXXX is aggressive after absorbing light.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Providing light sources with different intensity will make SCP-XXXX have distinct transformation. Press the button next to each light bulb, wait for SCP-XXXX changing its shape or behavior. However, it is not a suggested option, since SCP-XXXX needs to be contained within a 10cm x 10cm reinforced containment cube composed of non-reflective, high-density alloy.
